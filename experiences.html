Into the Deep

Discover the hidden trails of West Marin, guided by those who know the land best.

Curated Journeys

01

Muir Woods Dawn Hike

Experience the redwood cathedral before the crowds arrive. This exclusive early-access hike lets you walk in absolute silence, accompanied only by the distant ocean and the mist rolling through the branches. Includes a trailhead breakfast.

Duration: 3 Hours | Difficulty: Easy

02

Coastal Ridge Exploration

A challenging ascent up the coastal ridges offering unparalleled 360-degree views of the Pacific Ocean and Mount Tamalpais. Guided by our resident naturalist who illuminates the local flora and fauna.

Duration: 4.5 Hours | Difficulty: Hard

03

West Marin Artisans Trail

A gentle wander through the agricultural heart of Marin County. Visit local cheese makers, stop by wood-fire bakeries, and taste the bounty that inspires our kitchen at The Hearth.

Duration: 4 Hours | Difficulty: Easy
